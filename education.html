The TIP Breakdown

The Tuition Incentive Program (TIP) is a Michigan-specific scholarship for students with qualifying Medicaid history. For Payton, this is the key to a professional ranching education without the debt.

Phase Academic Goal What It Covers
Phase I Associate Degree or Certificate Full Tuition & Mandatory Fees (up to 80 credits)
Phase II Bachelor's Degree $500 per semester (max $2,000 total)

Pro Tip: To get the Phase II money, you must transfer to a 4-year school (like MSU) and start your Bachelor's within 30 months of finishing your Associate's.
