Choose Your Path

Path A: The Academic Specialist

Focus: Breeding, technical horsemanship, and facility management.

  • Use TIP for a 2-year MSU certificate.
  • Work as a vet tech or breeding assistant.
  • Ideal for long-term managerial stability.
Path B: The Working Wrangler

Focus: Guest relations, trail management, and outdoor tourism.

  • Skip formal college and head West for summer contracts.
  • Gain massive "hours in the saddle" experience.
  • Includes room and board (low expenses).
Path C: The Hybrid Strategy

Focus: The most well-rounded approach to ownership.

  • MSU in the winter, Wrangling in the summer.
  • Builds the fastest resume for bank loan officers.
  • Balances technical science with grit and field experience.
