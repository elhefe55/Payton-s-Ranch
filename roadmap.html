What if I don't get into MSU?

Start at Community College. It's cheaper, and MSU accepts almost all transfer credits from MI community colleges. You can still use TIP money!

What if I can't save $25,000?

Look into leasing land first. You can lease 10 acres for a few hundred dollars a month to start your business while you keep saving for a down payment.
